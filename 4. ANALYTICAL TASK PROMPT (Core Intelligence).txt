Perform a complete business analysis:

1. Trend Analysis
- Explain overall sales trend
- Identify growth or decline patterns
- Support with reasoning

2. Root Cause Analysis
- Explain WHY sales changed
- Correlate:
  - Promotions
  - Transactions
  - Seasonality
  - External factors

3. Customer Behavior Insights
- What does transaction data indicate?
- Are customers increasing or decreasing?

4. Promotion Effectiveness
- Are promotions driving sales?
- Is ROI of promotions justified?

5. Anomaly Detection Analysis
- Identify unusual spikes/drops
- Provide possible business reasons

6. Forecast Interpretation
- Explain future sales trend
- What does it mean for the business?

7. Risk Identification
- Highlight potential risks
- Example: declining demand, overstock, low engagement

8. Opportunity Identification
- Highlight growth opportunities
- Example: seasonal demand, high-performing products

9. Strategic Recommendations
Provide:
- Short-term actions (next 30 days)
- Medium-term strategy (3–6 months)
- Long-term improvements

10. Decision Support
- What should management DO next?
- Be direct and actionable
